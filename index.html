Research Experiences

  • Compound Screening

    2017.08-2017.11

    More than ten compounds were screened in neural stem cells, one of them are used on the rat to see its effects on neurogenesis. Now this particular compound is being studied on AD mice.

  • The neuroprotective effect of Zhengtian Pill (ZTP)

    2017.11-2018.04

    By treating KM mice with ZTP, LPS, Vitamin E and resveratrol, we found the antioxidative action and other positive effects of ZTP, some of the data have been published in 2019.

  • Neural stem cells (NSCs) transplantation in AD mice

    2017.08-2019.03

    Through injected NSCs in the brain of AD mice, we found decreased neurofibrillary tangles and improved learning memory. The protemics results is under verifying now.

  • The interaction between β-secretase and zinc finger protein

    2018.06-now

    My work is focus on two aspects: one is the interacting site between BACE1 promoter and zinc finger protein 335, another is the behavior tests and the electrophysiology of the special mouse model which is crossed by conditional knockout/knockin of ZNF335 mice and APP/PS1 mice.
